About:

The idea of whereislucy.net is an interactive puzzle that is also an LSD trip simulation.The objective of the site is for user to navigate through out the site and find hidden element that leads from one page to another, and by doing that user have to interact with all elements that appear on every single page to find out which one is the key to open the next page. I try to incorporate symptoms of LSD, such as dialated pupil and use that as a starting point to all my animation. I also took an attempts at mocking the culture, because LSD have been buy, sell and trade through the dark web. It is how the culture work and I wanted to keep that dark web aesthetic and poke fun at it with my corny music and corky looking gif. -Thomas Nguyen.
